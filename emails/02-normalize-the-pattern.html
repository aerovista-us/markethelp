Hi,
If you downloaded Interrupt the Loop, there is a good chance something in you already knows:
“I keep ending up in the same place.”
The same conversation.
The same feeling.
The same reaction.
The same regret afterward.
That does not mean you are failing.
Most emotional loops are protective patterns. At some point, they helped you survive, cope, avoid pain, stay connected, stay safe, or keep moving.
But what once protected you may now be keeping you stuck.
That is why the first step is not judgment.
The first step is noticing.
Before you respond, defend, explain, shut down, chase, avoid, or spiral, there is usually a tiny moment where your body knows something is happening.
That moment matters.
That is the doorway.
This work is not about becoming someone completely different. It is about learning to hear yourself clearly before the old pattern takes over.
Try this today:
When you feel activated, ask yourself:

“What am I trying to protect right now?”
Not “What is wrong with me?”

Not “Why am I like this?”

Just:

“What am I trying to protect?”
That question can soften the shame and bring you back to choice.
Warmly,
Cindy
