Hi,
Here is a gentle truth:
Sometimes comfort keeps us in the same loop.
Not because we are weak.
Because the familiar can feel safer than the unknown, even when the familiar is painful.
We know how to survive the old pattern.
We know the role we play.
We know when to go quiet.
We know when to over-explain.
We know when to make ourselves smaller.
We know how to keep the peace on the outside while abandoning ourselves on the inside.
But clarity asks something different from us.
Clarity asks:
What is this pattern costing me?
Not just emotionally.
But in my relationships.
In my body.
In my confidence.
In my sleep.
In my ability to trust myself.
That question can be uncomfortable.
But it can also be freeing.
Because once you can name the cost, you can begin choosing something different.
You do not have to change everything at once.
Start with one place where you already know the truth.
One conversation.
One boundary.
One pause.
One choice.
That is enough for today.
Warmly,
Cindy
